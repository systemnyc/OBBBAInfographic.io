The Human Impact: Who Gains and Who Loses?

Non-partisan analysis reveals a starkly regressive impact. When tax changes are combined with benefit cuts, the legislation results in a net financial loss for the poorest households, while the wealthiest see significant gains.

An Entrepreneur's Guide to the New Landscape

For business owners, the OBBBA creates a double-edged sword: powerful tax incentives for capital investment are coupled with significant risks from labor market instability and long-term economic uncertainty.

  • Explore Opportunity Zones: Defer and reduce capital gains taxes by investing in designated communities.
